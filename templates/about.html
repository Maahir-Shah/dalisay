Our Story
Established in 2018, the essence of Dalisay is intertwined with the word ‘PURE’. Dalisay was born out of the need of the hour - to unjunk the unhealthy without compromising on taste and making it nutritious. In today’s health-conscious environment, there has been a disconnect between pure, nutritious food and “sinful” delicious food, with many believing that they must choose one or the other. However, we decided to take a different approach - we wanted to provide our children with healthier alternatives to the junk food that they regularly consume, thereby bridging this gap without having to compromise on taste and flavour.
Though our ride had a few hiccups at first, trying to make unhealthy food both, fun and nutritious, we came up with a broad range of products that are not only relished by kids, but adults as well. This vision was realized with the conception of Dalisay - the perfect blend of the “Sinful” and the “Pure”.

With health being the top priority, we focus on using the best ingredients for our products, such as whole grains, dry fruits, and natural sweeteners free from preservatives. All our products are freshly prepared and can be customized for Gluten-free, Vegan or most dietary concerns. We currently operate from two locations in South Mumbai.
